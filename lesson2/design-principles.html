Contrast

Manhuako

manhuako.com

All fonts are easily readable on the website, background images have excellent contrast. The background contrasts nicely, and when resizing the elements snap to the right place without distortion.

Repetition

Netflix

Netflix.com

Carry out the repetition of an element more than once throughout a design in an organized and consistent way within its composition. It is also an important business tactic and company identity. Netflix's website acts as the face of how users perceive the company.

Proximity

Outlook

outlook.live.com

The design moves the elements closer together or further apart, they are using the principle of proximity. It also does this with the aspects of your images without losing the relationship between them, creating relevance, organization, structure, and hierarchy limiting clutter, and making the design more understandable.
