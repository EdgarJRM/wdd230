Target Audience and Scenarios

Target Audience

The target audience for this site is local businesses who want to join the chamber, and current members to obtain information such as community and chamber events, training, and policies.The site aims to meet the needs of companies and potential entrepreneurs who want to start or improve their own economic activity in the region. Create a way to build better business networks both locally and internationally. Provide information to those who are interested in finding the attractions and events of the city. It also supports government and non-profit organizations seeking additional resources or information from the State.

My first person is a new entrepreneur about 42 years old named Martín Quiroz. He is married and has 3 children. He has worked all his life for textile companies and with his experience, he has decided to start his own textile business. Martin wants to join the Chamber of Commerce to get involved in the community and have access to the group's benefits and expertise. He mainly uses his smartphone to access the website. He has no one to help him with digital marketing, he goes to the web to look for candidates or businesses that provide him with digital marketing services.

The second person is Guadalupe Ore, a 45 years old administrative manager of a large Saga Falabella corporation who wants to find training on topics important to the company, such as diversity/inclusion. In addition, he is looking for help to expand his networking presence to promote his business. She is married and has 1 daughter who is a teenager. She can access the website via phone as well as from her laptop.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

Martín seeks to make himself known in the network of members with the highest sales volume to offer his textile services.

Scenario 2:

Guadalupe is looking for training in the field of diversity/inclusion found at the chamber of commerce.

Scenario 3:

Martín wants to find a strategic location in the city to open his garment shop and wants to use the information provided by the chamber of commerce website.

Scenario 4:

Guadalupe, you want to connect with other local businesses and promote advertising space with them.

Scenario 5:

Guadalupe, investigate the calendar of activities to prepare discounts for those days and bring specific products, to boost your sales.
